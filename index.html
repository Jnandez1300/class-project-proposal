Class project proposal

Pong de la fury

Team Members:Julian Hernandez

Application Overview: Pongs de la fury:Based on the original game where players control paddles on either side of the screen, and they use these paddles on either side of the screen and they use the paddle to hit a ball back and fourth. The objective is to score points by causing the ball to pass the opponent's paddle without them being able to return it. It will go up until the score of 3.

Technology Overview

Proposal Last Updated 3/21/24

Feature List:Key features: Paddles:Players control paddles on either side of the screen which they use to hit the ball. Ball:a ball move back and fourth between the paddles and players must hit it with their paddles to keep it in play. Scoring:points are scored when a player hits the ball past their opponents paddle. Collision Detection: the fame includes collision detection to determine when the ball collides with a paddle or the boundaries of the screen. Speed variation:as the game progress the ball goes faster.
